Key Takeaways

In this section, we’ll review the key concepts covered in the course so far, including:

  • Black-box Testing: Focuses on system behavior without knowledge of the internal structure.
  • White-box Testing: Focuses on internal structures and logic, requiring knowledge of the code.
  • Experience-based Testing: Relies on the tester's experience and intuition to identify defects that might be missed by structured techniques.
