Experience-based Testing

Experience-based testing leverages the tester's experience and intuition to find defects. These types of defects might not be detected through systematic techniques like black-box or white-box testing.

Practical Example: Exploring an E-commerce Site

Below is a simple e-commerce product page. As an experience-based tester, explore the page and identify any potential usability issues or defects that a user might encounter.

Product: Cool Sneakers

Price: $79.99

Your Task

As an experience-based tester, consider the following:

  • Does the "Add to Cart" button work as expected?
  • Is the price clearly displayed and formatted correctly?
  • Are there any UI/UX issues that a user might encounter?

Identify any issues you find based on your experience and knowledge of what makes a good user experience.
